How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email that contains tracking information so you will know precisely when to expect your package. This email is automated, no need to reach out for tracking info, you will get it as soon as your order has shipped out. Be sure to whitelist onlineordersnoreply@novabrandprojection.com to receive communication about your order. If your order hasn't arrived and it is past the expected arrival date (please allow 10-14 business days for any produced on demand products), please reach out to customer service.
